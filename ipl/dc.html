Deccan Chargers also called as DC is a franchise that represents the city of Hyderabad in the Indian Premier League. They are the current holders of the IPL Trophy, having won the second season held in South Africa in 2009. The team is led by Adam Gilchrist and coached by Darren Lehmann, both former Australian Test and One-Day Cricketers. They finished last in the first season of the IPL, but emerged as the champions in the second season..
